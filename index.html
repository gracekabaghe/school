Activities

Music

The school offers piano lessons, violin, guitar and flute lessons. Students are encouraged to learn one or more instrument.

Art

Art lessons take place every Wednesday in the Lower Auditorium after school hours. All students who would like to join the program are encouraged to register at the school office.

Chess

The school chess club has seen some major regional wins in the past, students who cannot play chess yet should join the beginner class. Those who can play, should start working on the ranking system.

Golf

We are happy to say we have a mini golf course All students interested in playing golf should register at the admin office.

Dance

The dance club meets over the weekend in the school hall. Please be aware that, we have students from other schools who are part of this program.
